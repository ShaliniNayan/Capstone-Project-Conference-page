Welcome to the Sports League
 Conferance 2023

A joyful celebration believing in the value of openness and sharing, creating a positive change with people from different parts of the country.

Sports league is a unique initiative to promote all sports in region of India.The key objectives of this initiative are three fold: Firstly, to provide single platform to Sub Junior, Junior and Senior players (Professional & Amateurs) of all part of India to showcase their skill and talent. Secondly, to create future champions from all part of India. Thirdly, to make the sports talk of the town.The Sports League Conferance brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the Creative Commons affiliate network in a different parts of country every two years.This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Commons, free culture and open knowledge. So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge - free software, advocates, wikipedians, Open knowledge, alleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together.
